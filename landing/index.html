Core Features

FLAC in every room.

Choose your codec — FLAC, ALAC, Opus 320k, or AAC. Set bitrate, sample rate, and buffer size per channel. Monitor latency in real time. Every participant shows a live audio quality badge: codec, bitrate, sample rate, all visible at a glance.

Mix the room like a session.

Every participant is a track. Per-user channel strips with 3-band EQ, high-pass and low-pass filters with resonance, pan control, solo and mute, channel faders, a master crossfader, and record-to-file. Treat every voice channel like a mixing console.
